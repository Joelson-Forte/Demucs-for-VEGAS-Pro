  • Automatic detection of peaks, claps, and unique sound patterns
  • User-friendly interface for quick and accurate alignment

Benefits:

  • Significant time savings in post-production
  • High-precision synchronization without manual effort
  • Seamless Vegas Pro integration
  • Ideal for multi-camera setups, interviews, and events

Synchronization by Timecode

Designed for professional productions, this method uses timecode metadata to instantly and accurately synchronize audio and video files.

Features:

  • Supports standard timecode formats used in the industry
  • Fast processing of large media libraries

Benefits:

  • Rapid media synchronization for improved workflow
  • Excellent compatibility with professional cameras and audio recorders
  • Better file management in complex projects
